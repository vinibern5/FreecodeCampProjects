Fernando Masanori Ashikaga

Brazilian Python Teacher

Fernando Masanori is a known brazilian teacher. He teaches at FATEC São José dos Campos.
Masanori loves teaching, he is the creator of the first brazilian MOOC (Massive Online Course)
to teach programming, know as Python Para Zumbis that means "python for zombies" in english.
He chose to call his students zombies, because he thought that a newbie would rather be know
as a zombie than a newbie.He developed projects for Software Express, Cobra Technology,
Credicard Mastercard, PwC (PriceWaterhouseCoopers) and ITAÚ Bank Boston. His interests are
Python, Business Analytics, NoSQL. You can find Masanori social media at his about.me .
